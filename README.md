# 📖 Overview

Spotify Discovery Boost is an interactive product prototype that reimagines how users discover new music.

Instead of interrupting listening sessions with generic recommendations, the experience gives users complete control over **how much discovery** they want while clearly explaining **why** songs are recommended.

The goal is to improve trust, personalization, and user engagement without disrupting the listening experience.

---

# 🎯 Problem

Current recommendation experiences can feel:

- Interruptive
- Difficult to understand
- Hard to control
- Generic
- Invisible after feedback

Users often skip recommendations because they don't know:

- Why the song appeared
- Whether Spotify actually learned from their feedback
- How adventurous recommendations should be

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

Spotify Discovery Boost introduces a transparent discovery layer directly inside the player.

Instead of forcing recommendations, users decide how much exploration they want.

The system explains every recommendation, allows instant feedback, and visibly confirms personalization updates.

---

# ✨ Key Features

## 🎚️ Discovery Intensity

Choose your preferred recommendation style.

### 🌿 Subtle

- Adds **1** carefully matched recommendation
- Best for users who prefer familiar music

### 🌊 Balanced

- Adds **2** recommendations
- Mix of comfort-zone and fresh discoveries

### 🚀 Adventurous

- Adds **4** discovery tracks
- Includes **1 Weekend Pick**
- Designed for maximum exploration

---

## 💬 Transparent Recommendation Reasons

Every recommended song includes a clear explanation such as:

- Acoustic comfort zone
- Similar listening patterns
- Mood match
- Genre crossover
- Artist similarity

No more "recommended because Spotify said so."

---

## ❤️ Inline Personalization

Users can instantly respond to recommendations with:

- ❤️ Save
- 👍 More Like This
- 🚫 Skip Style

No hidden algorithms.

Immediate feedback.
